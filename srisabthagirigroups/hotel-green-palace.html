About Us

Experience comfort and luxury in the heart of Pondicherry

Hotel Green Palace is a centrally located hotel in Pondicherry, ideal for leisure and business travelers. Enjoy comfortable, spacious rooms, modern amenities, and warm hospitality in the heart of the city.

As a proud unit of Sri Sabthagiri Group, we uphold the highest standards of service, integrity, and guest satisfaction. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every stay with us is memorable and comfortable.

Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, our strategic location near the New Bus Stand makes it convenient to explore all that Pondicherry has to offer.
